The Fragrance

The olfactory family, also known as the fragrance family or scent family, refers to a categorization system used in the world of perfumery to classify individual notes based on their dominant aromatic characteristics. Understanding olfactory families can help you navigate the diverse world of fragrances and find scents that align with your preferences.
